Leetintelligence

    I founded LEETintelligence in 2020 because I am passionate about the possibilities for using location-based data. After graduating from the Asian Institute of Technology School of Environment, Resources and Development, Natural Resources Management in 2020, I was fortunate to find a postdoc position at Chulalongkorn University’s Urban and Regional Planning Department, where I have been using advance spatial location based intelligence, remote sensing and Drone technologies, and Google Earth Engine to Land Use, Land Use Change, and Forestry, urban dynamics, and carbon stock assessment in the tropics. When the COVID-19 virus emerged as a global pandemic, I saw yet another way location-based data can help people achieve their goals. If you have an idea for using location-based data, I would like to hear it.

    Manjunatha Venkatappa.
    Founder, LEETintelligence
